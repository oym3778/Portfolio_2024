About

WHO AM I???? Well, I'm a graduate from RIT with a degree in Game Design and a passion for Software Development. I went to RIT with a love for coding and since then that passion went from making games to anything software! I love the software process. I love getting a problem and figuring out the most efficient way to solve it. Especially if it's a hard one—it's like having a pickle jar and everyone's trying to open it but can't, and then you come around and BOOM, it's open and everyones super impressed... anyway...

I've spent the past 4 years trying to learn all I can about software and finally I want to put that work to use. Here is my portfolio! Stay a bit and check out some of the projects I'm working on :). If you're an employer or someone stopping by to visit, feel free to reach out—I'd love to talk about any of my projects or even this portfolio. Thanks for stopping by!
